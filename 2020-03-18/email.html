The Future of OSAI

Howdy open source developers!

We hope you’re enjoying spring break, and staying safe among other things. As I’m sure most of you know, the university has decided to effectively shut down campus in response to the arrival of COVID-19. In accordance with their new policies, this unfortunately means we will have to cancel all remaining in person activities for the club. We feel terrible that things are turning out this way, but there is hope! We have decided that we can continue club activities in a remote environment through the use of Zoom and our discord.

As part of this transition, we will be translating all future workshops into documented DIY style tutorials that we will publish online, and we will continue the ongoing projects as is but through online meetups as well as on-your-own work. Some workshops we have in store are the OpenCV workshop, the Latex workshop, and another Git workshop. Additionally, we’ll be adding a few new projects with the convenience of online correspondence and free time a lot of us have gained under the circumstances. We expect that for many people who weren’t previously attending meetings, this situation will also appeal to them to engage more because they can do so right from home. If you have any questions about the future of OSAI, feel free to ask. This transition will probably be a walk in the park for all of you gamers out there.

With that said, things will be operated somewhat more decentralized, but still active. I encourage everyone to take a more active role in the Discord if they want to continue an active role in the organization! We will resume club activities starting after spring break.

We hope you enjoy the remainder of vacation,
