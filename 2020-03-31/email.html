OSAI - Coming This Week!

Hey everyone!

We hope you’re all doing well and are staying safe during these (admittedly bizarre) times! We’ve got some new club developments that you folks will be interested in:

First up on the menu: we’ll be hosting our first Coding Hours session this upcoming Friday (04-03-2020) from 1pm-2pm CST! These hours will be an opportunity for you to get some programming done for club projects or workshops, while still being able to keep in touch with your clubmates. After this Friday, we’ll be hosting them every weekday from 1pm-2pm CST through Zoom, so come on out and join us!

Secondly, we’ll be hosting our first online social this Saturday (04-04-2020) from 8pm to 10pm CST! We'll be meeting up over Zoom, and we’ll livestream music with breakout rooms for online games, chatting, live drawing, and more!

And as always, we will still have our normal club meeting this Saturday from 1pm to 3pm CST.

We look forward to seeing your faces at our upcoming events! Until then, stay safe, and may you be blessed with as much toilet paper as your heart desires.
